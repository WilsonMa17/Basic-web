Enjoy and Relax

Tired of the bustling city life of urban Tokyo? If so, you can head down south of the Tokyo to reach the Hakone area. Only about half an hour by bullet train, many choose to go for a fun day of relaxation and to get away from their normal busy lives. It is close to Tokyo and also close to Mt.Fuji as well!

    How to get there:

  • From Shinjuku: Hop onto the limited express train or express train in Shinjuku Station. It will take 1 hour and 30 minutes to the Hakone-Yumoto Station.

  • From Tokyo: Taking a bullet train will take 30 minutes to Odawara Station from Tokyo or Shinagawa Station.

  • From Haneda Airport: If you wish to head to Hakone directly from the airport, taking the highway bus will take approximately 2 hours and 30 minutes.

Once you're there

Enjoy traditional inns and hotels by the mountainsides known for their hot springs, or locally called onsens. Eat local delicious cuisine and visit local traditional shrines. Take part in seasonal events and pay tributes at temples. Countless day tours with sightseeing options and boat travel also available for adventurous tourists.
