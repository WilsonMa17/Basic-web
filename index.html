Easy and Efficient

Japan is one of the world's top travel destination. Tokyo, the capital of Japan is often frequented because of its vast amount of activities and points of interests. Tokyo is very large and contains many cities and areas that is difficult for a beginner to navigate. There are big major cities as well as smaller ones. Each provide different experiences and different sightseeing activities. From ancient temple shrines to modern highrise buildings, Tokyo blends the old with the new and is a welcome place for anyone.

https://www.pasmo.co.jp/

Who said getting around in Japan is difficult? Upon landing in Japan, you can purchase a card called PASMO. You can fill up the card with amounts of money and use the card as an "all-in-one", you can use it for shopping, for transportation and for miscellaneous fares. It is seamless and easy to use, just use it on a scanner in places that accepts PASMO, which is pretty much almost every store and kiosk. You can use it at convienence stores, public transportation, and shops as well. Just beep and go, easy and simple.
