Expedia

Within central Tokyo,there are many cities and tourist spots that offers different unique experiences and different activities. This dense urban location contains endless buildings and structures that will sure to awe you. For anyone looking for the latest in technology and japanese gaming/manga culture, they can head over to Akihabara. Fashionistas can head over to Harajuku and dive deep into clothing stalls and clothing stores. Shinjuku and Shibuya offers great nightlife and endless activities.


SHIBUYA

Shibuya crossing, one of the world's largest gathering in crosswalk sections. Shibuya is a perfect place for shopping and exploring. Walkability is a goal of the city planners, closing off streets to encourage more pedestrian traffic and to reduce automobile interference. You can explore by foot and enter countless alleyways and streets as you soak in the bustling life of Japan. After a while of exploring, you can always find a local restaurant that offers delicious and healthy foods at an affordable price.

AKIHABARA

An electronic and gaming culture paradise, Akihabara is home to the latest technology,gaming, and Japanese entertainment. This is the place to go if you want to play in arcade centers that offers hundreds of gaming machines not found any where else, stock up on the latest toys and figures, and to get lost in the multi-floor buildings. This city is dedicated to gaming, entertainment, animation, and manga. Hundreds of buildings with over 10 floors of shops is filled with the latest CDs, DVDs, and video games. Each floor level is sure to provide a new discovery and offers different merchandises.
